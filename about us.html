Welcome to Zoom – Your Trusted Solar Partner At zoom, we are passionate about promoting the use of renewable energy sources and helping people harness the power of the sun to create a more sustainable future. Our Story Solar Energy was founded in 2010 by a group of solar energy enthusiasts who shared a common vision – to create a world where everyone can access clean, sustainable energy.
